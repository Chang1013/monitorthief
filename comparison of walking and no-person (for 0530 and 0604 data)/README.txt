The version just means that the data used is different.

version1 : choose 0604 walking data (RT_version2 and TR_version2) and 0530 no-person data (nobody_version1)

version2 : choose 0604 walking data (RT_version3 and TR_version3) and 0530 no-person data (nobody_version2)

version3 : choose 0604 walking data (RT_version4 and TR_version4) and 0530 no-person data (nobody_version3)

-------------------------
TR means walk from transmitter to receiver
RT means walk from receiver to transmitter
